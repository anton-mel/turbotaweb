TurBOTa for Chrome

Check the cruelty-free status of the brands faster with TurBOTa for Chrome! Developed with love by NGO ESMU team!

Add to Chrome Add to Safari

This extension goes through the web page you open, finds brands on it, and shows whether these brands test their products on animals or not. It takes just one second, and you already know the cruelty-free status of a particular brand from the notification in the bottom right corner of your screen. You can also easily turn off/on all our functions whenever you want.

For now, we use a database of 600+ brands and plan to enlarge it with new updates. So, if the brand you are looking for is missing in our database, you can request to add it through the expansion's popup.

Website developed by NGO ESMU in 2022, Ukraine
